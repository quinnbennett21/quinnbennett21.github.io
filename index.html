Biography

Quinn Rhoda, Esq.

Quinn Rhoda is an Intellectual Property lawyer focusing on patent and trademark matters. Mr. Rhoda's practice includes Intellectual Property portfolio management and development. Mr. Rhoda counsels clients in a wide variety of technical areas including computer systems and software applications, telephony and computer networks, medical devices, lighting systems, materials and manufacturing processes, complex data management systems, electronics, energy management systems and energy storage including fuel cells, mechanical devices and consumer products.  Mr. Rhoda studied law at Suffolk University Law School. 

His Approach

The Client Always Comes First

Mr. Rhoda strives to simplify your ongoing challenges by providing integrated legal solutions for you, your family, and your business. He devotes himself                            wholeheartedly to excellent client service and promise to give you and your business personalized attention from a highly skilled group of dedicated attorneys and professional staff. Mr. Rhoda has the experience to guide you and your business through the many opportunities and challenges of today's constantly changing global economy. 

He offers practical advice and planning tips so you can anticipate and avoid problems before they occur. He also helps you troubleshoot and devise sensible solutions when confronted with unexpected situations that frequently arise during the ups and downs of the business cycle. 

His loyal clients view him as their single-source, long-term strategic partner for all of their legal needs, their proactive legal advisors and trusted navigators for life. 

Looking ahead, as he continues to evolve, we pledge to remain true to our founding principles of providing the highest level of client service, cost-effective and sound legal advice, and excellence in courtroom advocacy. Indeed, our ultimate and primary goal is your success.
